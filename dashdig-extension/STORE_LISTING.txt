Dashdig - Humanize and Shortenize URLs

Turn cryptic, ugly URLs into clean, memorable links powered by AI.

BEFORE: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08N5WRWNW?ref=cm_sw_r_cp_api_i_dl
AFTER:  dashdig.com/Wireless.Earbuds.Deal

KEY FEATURES

One-Click Shortening
Click the extension icon, paste any URL, and hit "Dig This!" to instantly create a human-readable short link.

Current Tab URL
Grab the URL of your current browser tab with a single click. No copy-paste needed.

Right-Click to Shorten
Right-click any link on any webpage and select "Shorten with Dashdig" for instant shortening.

QR Code Generation
Generate downloadable QR codes for your shortened URLs. Perfect for sharing in presentations, print materials, or mobile.

Recent Links History
Access your last 10 shortened URLs directly in the extension. Click to open, or copy to share again.

Clipboard Integration
One-click copy with visual confirmation. Your shortened URL is ready to paste anywhere.

Daily Counter Badge
See how many links you've shortened today with the badge counter on the extension icon.

WHY DASHDIG?

Unlike random character URLs (bit.ly/3x7Kq2), Dashdig creates meaningful, memorable links that humans can actually read and remember. Our AI analyzes your URL and generates a semantic slug that describes the content.
